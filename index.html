How it works

Everything automated.

Here is exactly what happens from the moment you import your bank data to the moment your dashboard is ready.

01

Import your bank data

Upload any bank CSV file — HDFC, ICICI, SBI, Chase, Barclays, Wise, Payoneer or any other. You can also load sample data instantly. No template needed — the system auto-detects your date, amount, and description columns regardless of how your bank formats them.

02

6-stage automated pipeline

Your data passes through 6 automated stages: Ingest → Normalize → Classify → FX Convert → Tax Compute → Dashboard. Each transaction gets auto-classified (Uber → Travel, Adobe → Software, etc.) using ML keyword rules with a confidence score from 1–5.

03

Everything computed for you

Instantly see: total income, expenses, net profit, 3-month smoothed income average, quarterly tax estimate, cash flow forecast, expense breakdown by category, and project profitability. All calculated automatically from your imported data. Nothing to fill in manually.

Income Smoothing

Freelancers get paid irregularly — ₹1.2L one month, ₹30K the next. Income Smoothing calculates a 3-month rolling average of your income and plots it as a line over your raw monthly bars. This tells you your real, stable monthly earning power — not just what happened to arrive this month. Use this number to set your personal monthly budget.

Computed automatically from your transactions. No input needed.

Tax Estimator — Latest Government Rules

Calculates your quarterly advance tax using the latest official tax slabs sourced from government portals. For India: Finance Act 2025 — New Regime (nil tax up to ₹12L via 87A rebate, ₹75K standard deduction) vs Old Regime. Also covers USA (IRS 2025), UK (HMRC 2025-26), Canada, Australia, Germany, France, Singapore. Shows quarterly due dates, effective rate, monthly set-aside needed, and deduction hints based on your actual expense categories.

IncomeWave Documentation

Complete guide to every feature — what it does, how it works, and how to use it.

What is IncomeWave?
IncomeWave is an automated financial dashboard for freelancers and gig workers. It solves the specific problems freelancers face that general accounting tools like Wave or FreshBooks don't address: irregular income (smoothing), quarterly advance tax scheduling, multi-currency client payments, and project-by-project profitability. Everything is automated from a single CSV import — no manual data entry, no spreadsheets.
Who is it for?
Any independent worker: freelancers, consultants, designers, developers, content creators, Upwork/Fiverr/Toptal workers, coaches, photographers, writers. Especially useful if you: get paid irregularly (not on the 1st of every month), have clients in multiple countries/currencies, aren't sure how much to set aside for advance tax, or want to know which clients are most profitable.
Income Smoothing — how it works
After importing your transactions, IncomeWave groups income by month. For each month, it computes the 3-month rolling average (current month + 2 prior months ÷ 3). This smoothed value is plotted as a line over your actual monthly bars. The smoothed figure is your budgeting baseline — what you can reliably spend monthly, ignoring lucky big months and scary empty months.
Tax Engine — India FY 2025-26
New Regime (Finance Act 2025): ₹0–4L: 0% · ₹4–8L: 5% · ₹8–12L: 10% · ₹12–16L: 15% · ₹16–20L: 20% · ₹20–24L: 25% · ₹24L+: 30%. Standard deduction ₹75,000. Sec 87A rebate: nil tax up to ₹12L. Cess: 4%.

Old Regime: ₹0–2.5L: 0% · ₹2.5–5L: 5% · ₹5–10L: 20% · ₹10L+: 30%. Allows 80C (₹1.5L), 80D (₹25K), HRA, LTA.
